Chocalate Cake

""Here is the fast and easy version of the classic Chocalate Cake Ricipe. Enjoy!""


Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black Chocalate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the Chocalate cake and butter together

2

Mix the Sugar and Eggs

3

Mix everything to get smooth dough

4

Bake in the Oven and do this do that to get a very long text
